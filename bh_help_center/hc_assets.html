Understanding the New Help Center

Do you know what one of the number one things asked in Live Chats or phone calls to Customer Service is? If you guessed, "Where is my order?", you would be right. But our customers' questions don't end there. Because B&H ships worldwide, we deal with many complex situations. The international section of the current Help Center is frequently clicked. We want our Help Center to be truly helpful.
