Intro & Guide

Understanding the New Help Center

Do you know what one of the number one things asked in Live Chats or phone calls to Customer Service is? If you guessed, "Where is my order?", you would be right. But our customers' questions don't end there. Because B&H ships worldwide, we deal with many complex situations. The international section of the current Help Center is frequently clicked. We want our Help Center to be truly helpful.

Some Aspects of the New Help Center

  1. Hiearchy by Frequency: Based on stats, the most clicked items appear at the top and the least clicked follow in order below. Also size is used to display importance, with biggest items on top.

  2. Reorganize the Architecture: Particular subjects and topics have been either rewritten, combined with other topics or deleted. This restructure is part of updating and consolidation copy from the old Help Center.

  3. Take Advantage of the New Design & Wider Size: We want to be responsive to new larger monitors and respective of smaller screens. The most important information is all on top.

  4. New Ways of Finding Answers: Based on a lot current trends, customers are increasily finding answer through videos and Frequently Answered Questions.

  5. More Enjoyable: A new look and some mirco interactions that brings the Help Center to life.
